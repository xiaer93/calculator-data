## 计算公式
- This salary calculator assumes the hourly and daily salary inputs
            to be unadjusted values. All other pay frequency inputs are
            assumed to be holidays and vacation days adjusted values. This
            calculator also assumes 52 working weeks or 260 weekdays per year
            in its calculations. The unadjusted results ignore the holidays
            and paid vacation days.
