## 计算公式
- This calculation presents potential savings plans based on desired savings at retirement.
- This calculation estimates the amount a person can withdraw every month in retirement.
- Some experts claim that savings of 15 to 25 times of a person's current annual income are enough to last them throughout their retirement. Of course, there are other ways to determine how much to save for retirement. The calculations here can be helpful, as can many other retirement calculators out there. It also can be helpful to speak with licensed professionals who help people plan their retirements.
- Our Retirement Calculator can help by considering inflation in several calculations. Please visit the Inflation Calculator for more information about inflation or to do calculations involving inflation.
- For more information about or to do calculations involving pensions, please visit the Pension Calculator.
