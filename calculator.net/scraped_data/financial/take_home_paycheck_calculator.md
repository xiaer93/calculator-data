## 计算公式
- Use this calculator to estimate the actual paycheck amount that is brought home after taxes and deductions from salary. It can also be used to help fill steps 3 and 4 of a W-4 form. This calculator is intended for use by U.S. residents. The calculation is based on the 2026 tax brackets and the new W-4, which reflect the changes introduced by the One Big Beautiful Bill.
- Social Security is a federal government system in the U.S. that provides monetary benefits to retired, unemployed, or disabled people, paid for largely by society. The Social Security tax rate is 6.20% (total including employer contribution: 12.40%) up to an annual maximum of $184,500 for 2026 ($176,100 for 2025). For more information about or to do calculations involving Social Security, please visit the Social Security Calculator.
